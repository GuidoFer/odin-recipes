Salpicon de Pollo

Salpicon de pollo is a delicious vegetable salad with chicken and mayonnaise. It is one of the 4 recipes that we have prepared for you

INGREDIENTS

  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Pea
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Chicken
  • Mayonesa

PREPARATION

  1. Peel the potatoes, carrots, peas and cook them.
  2. Cook a chicken breast
  3. Cut into squares the potatoes, carrots, tomatoes and chicken
  4. Strip the lettuce and wash it
  5. Put the previous ingredients in a container and add mayonnaise to taste.
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Mix all the ingredients
  8. Put the lettuce leaves on the plate to serve and put the salad
